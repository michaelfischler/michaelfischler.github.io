Equity Release

Equity Release is a growing market which allows those home-owners over the age of 55 to release some of the money tied up in their property.

There are pages to help you understand the different schemes and options, risks involved, safeguards and guarantees built in and guidance on how much you can borrow.

Mortgages

For those looking to purchase a home, you may research the initial set up costs of buying a house and the processes involved.

Also included is a fully functioning mortgage searching facility giving up to the minute products and rates available across the UK, including first time buyer deals, mortgages for home purchase, remortgages and buy to let properties.

Protection

The financial impact of a life crisis can be devastating, whether it is through a death, serious or long term illness or redundancy. Life insurance and income replacement plans can help relieve the financial effects of such events, and ensure life can still be as comfortable as possible afterwards.

Welcome to Big Dicks Incorporated Ltd, a specialist financial adviser company based in Lincoln, Lincolnshire. The company specialises in providing Equity Release advice and plans for the retired, arranging Mortgages of all kinds and advising on and arranging Life Insurance & Income Protection for individuals, business owners and families.

The company is owned and managed by Andy Wilson, a well-respected and experienced financial adviser. Andy is building on over 34 years’ experience of providing his clients in Lincolnshire and across the East Midlands with sound financial planning.
